Pasta Recipe

Description

Spaghetti alla puttanesca is an Italian pasta dish invented in Naples in the mid-20th century and made typically with tomatoes, olive oil, olives, anchovies, chili peppers, capers, and garlicâ€”with vermicelli or spaghetti pasta.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Sunrise tomatoes
  • Black olives
  • Capers
  • Anchovy
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Cook pasta to al dente
  2. Chop up black olives, capers and anchovy
  3. In a large pan, add butter, black olives, capers and anchovy, cook it for a few minutes until you smell the aroma
  4. add pasta and little bit of pasta water
